Backstory

It all began when I started working at an organization two years ago as an intern..

I was tasked to create and name folders according to the number of expenses of that month It was a bunch ranging in the hundreds. And it took over an hour to finalize, the correct names, amounts, currencies, invoice numbers, etc.. I wouldn't say it was difficult but definetly boring. And I could've instead done more productive stuff.
I just knew there was an easier way to automate that task.. but i didn't know how but I was curious enough to search for the answer. I used excel to print MDs (Make directorate) for each entry and pasted them into a bat file to execute.
And boom. this whole thing took about 8 seconds.
And so I thought of better ways to build it more efficiently and I discovered the power of python.
From then on I took the cs50x course which taught me most of what I know now about algorithms and how computers work.

I had thought of programming before but never really understood why people do it and how they find joy doing it. But now that I know the reason, I cannot even think of stopping.

That's when I realized, I love problem solving.
And in turn, programming.

Back when I thought of myself as "The king of excel"
